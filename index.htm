Building ourselves a safety net
in the time of Covid-19

Reaching out to build connections with our neighbors, in order to support them and ourselves as we deal with the effects of the Covid-19 epidemic.

To join: email north.crown.hill@gmail.com

What is it?

Knowing our neighbors

Getting connected to our neighbors, including the non-internet-enabled among us

Supporting each other

Person-to-person support and mutual aid, physically and psychologically

Communication

A Slack chat app for real-time sharing of needs, news, and support for each other

Email heartbeat

Occasional group updates via email newsletter
